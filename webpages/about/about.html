Who We Are

A Place Where Innovation Meets Purpose

Founded in 2014, Yuvalay is a charitable trust dedicated to empowering youth through education, leadership, creativity and innovation. Over the years it has evolved into one of Gujarat's growing innovation ecosystems where students, makers, entrepreneurs and professionals collaborate to solve real-world challenges.

At Yuvalay, learning extends beyond classrooms. Through workshops, mentorship, community initiatives and the MakerSpace, every learner gets opportunities to design, prototype and transform ideas into impactful solutions.
