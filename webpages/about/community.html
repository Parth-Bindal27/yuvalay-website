People of Yuvalay

Built by curious minds and courageous builders.

At Yuvalay Makerspace, innovation thrives because of the people who turn ideas into action. Founders, educators, mentors, volunteers, and learners come together to shape a future that is generous, practical, and deeply human.

Why people matter

Our mission grows through the people who believe in it.

Since 2014, Yuvalay has expanded from a simple idea into a living ecosystem of education, making, leadership, and service. What makes it extraordinary is not only the programs, but the people who make each experience meaningful.

From trustees shaping direction to mentors guiding confidence, from volunteers building community to learners discovering purpose, every role adds to the strength of the institution.
