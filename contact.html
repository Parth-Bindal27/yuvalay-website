Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the questions we receive most often.

Who can join Yuvalay?

Anyone passionate about innovation, education, volunteering, social impact, technology, or community development is welcome to join.

How can I volunteer?

Fill out the contact form or email us. Our team will guide you through the onboarding process.

Do you conduct workshops?

Yes. Yuvalay regularly organizes innovation, robotics, coding, design thinking, entrepreneurship, and STEM workshops.

Can organizations partner with Yuvalay?

Absolutely. We actively collaborate with educational institutions, NGOs, startups, corporates, and community organizations.

How can I donate?

Donations can be made through our official website. Every contribution supports education, innovation, and youth empowerment initiatives.

Where is Yuvalay located?

Our Makerspace is located in Vadodara, Gujarat, India. Please check the map above for directions.
